Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a website take to build?

Single-page websites take 1–2 weeks. Multi-page websites (4–8 pages) take 3–6 weeks. Large or custom websites can take 6–12 weeks. We provide a clear timeline during the Discovery phase.

Can I update the website myself?

Yes, except for the Starter package. We provide a CMS or short tutorial for content management so you can handle updates easily.

What happens after launch?

We offer post-launch support, bug fixes, improvements, and optional maintenance packages to ensure your site stays healthy.

Can I request custom features?

Yes. We can integrate booking systems, payment gateways, APIs, advanced forms, and more depending on your business needs.

Do I need to provide my own hosting and domain?

If you already have them, we can use your setup. Otherwise, we will recommend suitable options and handle everything for you.
